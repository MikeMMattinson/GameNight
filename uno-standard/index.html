UNO with Standard Playing Cards

Players: 2–6

Deck: 2 standard 52-card decks + 4 jokers (optional)

Time: 20–40 minutes

Ages: 7+

Type: Card / Matching / Variant

🎯 Objective

Same as UNO: Be the first to play all your cards by matching color (suit) or number (rank), using action cards to disrupt opponents.

🎨 Card Color Mapping

  • ♥ Hearts = Red
  • ♦ Diamonds = Yellow
  • ♣ Clubs = Green
  • ♠ Spades = Blue

🔁 Matching Rules

  • On your turn, play a card that matches either the color (suit) or number (rank) of the top card.
  • If you can't play, draw 1 card from the pile. If playable, you may play it.

🃏 Number Cards Mapping

  • Ace = 1
  • 2 through 9 = same
  • 10 = treated as 0
  • Jack, Queen, King = Action Cards

⚡ Action Cards

  • Jack = Skip → Next player loses their turn
  • Queen = Reverse → Reverses turn order (acts as Skip in 2-player game)
  • King = Draw Two → Next player draws 2 cards and misses a turn
  • Joker = Wild → Choose any suit/color
    Optional: If no matching card, play Joker
    Add 2nd Joker set for Wild Draw Four → Next player draws 4 & skips turn (optional)

📢 Special Rules

  • When you have only one card left, you must say "UNO!" or draw 2 penalty cards if caught.
  • No playable card? Draw 1.
  • No stacking unless allowed by house rules.

🏁 Ending a Round

  • First to play all cards ends the round.
  • Remaining cards count as penalty points (optional scoring):
    • Number cards = face value
    • J/Q/K = 20 points
    • Jokers = 50 points

🏆 Ending and Winning

  • First to win a set number of rounds, or
  • First to 500 points if tracking scores

📝 Notes

  • For best experience: use 2 full decks + 4 Jokers
  • Optional: Use red Jokers for Wild, black Jokers for Wild Draw Four
  • Consider printing a quick-reference card for new players
