UNO

Players: 2–10

Time: 15–30 minutes

Ages: 7+

Type: Card / Matching / Family

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to score 500 points by discarding all your cards and earning points from opponents' remaining cards.

🧰 Setup

  1. Shuffle the 108-card deck and deal 7 cards to each player.
  2. Place the rest face-down as the draw pile.
  3. Flip the top card to start the discard pile (apply action effect if needed).
  4. Player to dealer’s left goes first; play proceeds clockwise.

🃏 Card Types

  • Number cards: 0–9 in red, green, blue, yellow
  • Action cards (same colors):
    • Skip: next player loses their turn
    • Reverse: reverses turn order
    • Draw Two: next player draws 2 and misses a turn
  • Wild cards (black):
    • Wild: choose a new color
    • Wild Draw Four: choose a color and next player draws 4 cards
      (only if you have no matching colors)

🔁 Gameplay

  • Play a card that matches the discard pile by number, color, or symbol.
  • If you can’t play, draw one card. If it’s playable, you may play it immediately.
  • Optional: you may draw even if you have a playable card.
  • Use action and wild cards to change the flow.
  • When you have one card left, you must shout “UNO!” before ending your turn.
    • If caught failing to do so, draw 2 penalty cards.

🏁 Ending a Round

  • Round ends when one player discards their last card.
  • That player scores based on cards left in opponents’ hands:
    • Number cards = face value
    • Action cards = 20 points
    • Wild cards = 50 points

🏆 Ending and Winning

  • First player to reach 500 points wins
  • Or: play multiple rounds and declare the winner by:
    • Most rounds won
    • Lowest total score

📝 Notes

  • Use house rules to customize stacking and draw penalties
  • For quicker games, reduce the point goal (e.g. 200 or 300)
  • Challenge Rule (optional):
    • If you suspect a Wild Draw Four was played illegally, challenge it!
    • If the player was bluffing, they draw 4 cards instead of you.
