Silent UNO

Players: 2–10
Time: 15–30 minutes
Ages: 8+
Type: Card / Stealth Variant / No Talking

OBJECTIVE

Be the first player to play all your cards — but without saying a word, including “UNO!”

RULE CHANGES FROM CLASSIC UNO

  1. NO TALKING ALLOWED
    No verbal communication, gestures, or mouthing words. No calling “UNO.”
  2. SILENT UNO RULE
    When you have 1 card left, silently place a special card face-down in front of you as your secret UNO signal.
  3. CHALLENGING SILENT UNO
    If someone has 1 card and gave no signal, “buzz” them.
    Correct = they draw 2 cards. Incorrect = you draw 2 cards.
  4. ACTION CARDS STILL APPLY
    All classic action cards are used — silently.
  5. BREAKING SILENCE = PENALTY
    If anyone speaks or signals, they draw 2 penalty cards.

ENDING AND WINNING

First to discard all cards wins the round.

  • Use standard UNO scoring, or tally wins

NOTES

  • Use a token card as silent UNO signal
  • Great for family, classroom, or camp
