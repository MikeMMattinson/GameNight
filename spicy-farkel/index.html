Spicy Farkel Dice Game

Manufactured by: LGI – Legendary Games

Players: 2–6

Time: 30–45 minutes

Ages: 8+

Type: Dice / Press-your-luck / House Rule Variant

🎯 Objective

Be the first player to score 10,000 points by rolling high-risk, high-reward combinations.

🧰 Setup

  1. You need 6 standard dice.
  2. Each player needs a scoresheet and pencil.
  3. Choose starting player randomly (e.g. highest roll).
  4. Play proceeds clockwise.

🎲 Gameplay

  • Roll all 6 dice on your turn.
  • Set aside at least one scoring die or combo each roll.
  • You may stop and “bank” points at any time.
  • If you roll no scoring dice, it’s a Farkle – you score 0 that turn.
  • If all 6 dice score, you have hot dice and may roll all 6 again.

💯 Scoring Combinations

  • Single 1 = 100 points
  • Single 5 = 50 points
  • Three 1s = 1000 points
  • Three 2s–6s = 100 × face value (e.g. three 3s = 300)
  • Four of a kind = 2× three-of-a-kind value
  • Five of a kind = 3× three-of-a-kind value
  • Six of a kind = 3000 points
  • Straight (1–6) = 1500 points
  • Three pairs = 1500 points
  • Four of a kind + a pair = 1500 points
  • Two triplets = 2500 points

🔥 Spicy Color Bonus

If a scoring combination includes at least one colored die, apply the Spicy Bonus:

  • +500 bonus points to that combo.

🌶️ Spicy Penalty (optional)

If you Farkle and at least one of the dice is colored:

  • Subtract 500 points from your total score (can't go below 0).

📌 Examples

  • Three 3s with one red die = 300 + 500 = 800 points
  • Straight (1–6) with 2 red dice = 1500 + 500 = 2000 points
  • Farkle with 2 colored dice = -500 points (if using penalty rule)

🌶️ Spicy Rules

  • Spicy Farkle Penalty: 3 consecutive Farkles = -1000 points
  • Spicy Bonuses:
    • Five 1s = 2000 points
    • Six 5s = 2500 points
    • Double Farkle (no score after hot dice reroll) = lose all points for that round

🧮 Getting on the Board

You must score at least 1000 points in a single turn to start banking points. (Spicier than regular Farkle!)

🏁 Ending and Winning

  • First to reach 10,000+ triggers the final round.
  • All others get one last turn.
  • Highest final score wins.

📝 Notes

  • Perfect for risk-takers who love punishment & reward!
  • Customize with bonus combos or wild dice for extra spice.
