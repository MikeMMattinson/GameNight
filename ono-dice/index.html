Bank Dice Game

Players: 2–6

Time: 15–30 minutes

Ages: 10+

Type: Dice / Digital Banking / Press-your-luck

🎯 Objective

Earn the most virtual money by rolling dice and choosing when to bank your gains before going bust.

🧰 Setup

  1. Each player downloads and opens the Bank app.
  2. Create a game and invite others using a join code or QR.
  3. Each player starts with $0.
  4. Decide on a win condition:
    • First to reach $50,000
    • Or play a fixed number of rounds (e.g. 10 rounds)

🎲 Gameplay

  • On your turn, tap “Roll” in the app to roll the dice.
  • Dice results award cash or may trigger penalties.
  • After each roll, choose to:
    • Roll again to try earning more
    • Bank your total and end your turn safely
  • If you roll a bust symbol (💣, ☠️, etc.), you lose all money from that turn.

💸 Bank Symbols (App Theme May Vary)

  • 💵 = Gain $1000
  • 💣 = Bust (lose all unbanked money)
  • 🏦 = Bonus bank symbol (safe cash or multiplier)
  • 🧲 = Multiplier or reroll bonus
  • Check the app’s symbol legend for theme-specific effects.

🏁 Ending and Winning

  • Game ends when a player reaches the set goal (e.g. $50,000)
  • Or after a set number of rounds (e.g. 10 rounds)
  • Highest banked total wins

📝 Notes

  • Only money you bank is safe.
  • Pushing your luck is how you win big—but also bust!
  • The app handles scorekeeping and round tracking.
  • Great for casual or remote play.

🔀 Variants

  • Team Mode: Add teammates' scores together for a group total
  • Double Trouble: Must roll at least twice before banking
  • Speed Bank: 10-second limit to make your decision each roll
