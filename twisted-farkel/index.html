Twisted Farkel 12

A dice game with 12 wild and twisted rules. Roll, twist, and try to survive!

TWISTED RULES TABLE (Roll 2d6 after first roll each turn)

  • 2 – BANK ROBBER: Steal 250 points from another player
  • 3 – DOUBLE TROUBLE: Roll twice before banking
  • 4 – BLIND ROLL: Close eyes, no re-rolls
  • 5 – PASS THE PAIN: Force someone else to roll immediately
  • 6 – BUST INSURANCE: Farkel = keep half your points
  • 7 – REVERSE ORDER: Change play direction
  • 8 – SUDDEN DEATH: Must keep rolling until Hot Streak or Farkel
  • 9 – STICKY DICE: Must keep all scoring dice from first roll
  • 10 – POINT SWAP: Swap scores with player to your right
  • 11 – WILD BONUS: 1s and 5s worth double this turn
  • 12 – JACKPOT: Gain +500 bonus points this turn
