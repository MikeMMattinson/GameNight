Yahtzee Dice Game

Players: 1–6

Time: 30–45 minutes

Ages: 8+

Type: Dice / Scoring / Strategy

🎯 Objective

Score the highest total points by rolling combinations of five dice over 13 rounds.

🧰 Setup

  1. Each player gets a scoresheet and pencil.
  2. Use 5 dice and a cup for rolling.
  3. Decide who goes first (e.g. highest single die roll).

🎲 Gameplay

  • You may roll the dice up to 3 times per turn.
  • After each roll, you may set aside dice to keep and reroll the rest.
  • After your final roll, choose an available scoring category to fill in.
  • Each category can be used only once.
  • Categories are grouped into Upper and Lower Sections.

🔢 Upper Section

  • Ones through Sixes: Score the total of only that number (e.g. 3 Fives = 15 pts)
  • Bonus: If the Upper Section total is 63 or more, score a 35-point bonus

🔽 Lower Section

  • 3 of a Kind: Total of all 5 dice if at least 3 are the same
  • 4 of a Kind: Total of all 5 dice if at least 4 are the same
  • Full House: 25 points (3 of one number and 2 of another)
  • Small Straight: 30 points (sequence of 4 numbers, e.g. 1-2-3-4)
  • Large Straight: 40 points (sequence of 5 numbers, e.g. 2-3-4-5-6)
  • Yahtzee: 50 points (5 of a kind)
  • Chance: Total of all 5 dice, any combination
  • Yahtzee Bonus: +100 points for each additional Yahtzee

🏁 Ending and Winning

  • After 13 rounds, total your Upper and Lower Section scores.
  • Highest overall score wins.
