Skunk Dice

A classic press-your-luck game played with five dice over five rounds (S, K, U, N, K).

HOW TO PLAY

  1. Each round starts with everyone standing.
  2. Roll 2 dice and decide to stay in or sit down.
  3. Rolling a 1 = lose points that round.
  4. Both dice show 1 = lose all points from the game.
  5. Last player standing wins highest score for that round.
