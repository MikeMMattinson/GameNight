Farkle Hot Streak

Players: 2–6

Time: 20–40 minutes

Ages: 8+

Type: Dice / Press-your-luck / Combo Scoring

🎯 Objective

Score the most points by chaining successful Hot Streaks — turns where all dice score — and activating powerful combo multipliers.

🧰 Features

  • 6 standard dice
  • Scorepad and pencils
  • Optional: Hot Streak combo cards or printed tracker sheet

🧮 Setup

  1. Each player rolls 1 die; highest goes first.
  2. All players start at 0 points.
  3. Play proceeds clockwise.

🎲 Gameplay Overview

  • Roll all 6 dice.
  • Set aside at least one scoring die or combo per roll.
  • You may reroll remaining dice as long as you score.
  • If all 6 dice score, you've completed a Hot Streak:
    • You may reroll all 6 dice and continue your turn.
    • Each new Hot Streak in the same turn increases your Combo Multiplier.
  • If no scoring dice are rolled: Farkle – lose all points from this turn, including combo bonuses.

💯 Scoring (Base Values)

  • Single 1 = 100 pts
  • Single 5 = 50 pts
  • Three 1s = 1000 pts
  • Three 2s–6s = 100 × face value
  • Four of a kind = 2× value of three-of-a-kind
  • Five of a kind = 3×
  • Six of a kind = 3000 pts
  • Straight (1–6) = 1500 pts
  • Three pairs = 1500 pts

🔥 Hot Streak Combos

  • 1st Hot Streak: normal scoring
  • 2nd Hot Streak: 2× points from that roll
  • 3rd Hot Streak: 3× points
  • 4th and beyond: 4× points for each additional Hot Streak roll
  • You may stop and bank your turn's total at any time

📈 Getting on the Board

You must score at least 1000 points in a single turn before you can bank points.

🏁 Ending and Winning

  • When a player reaches 10,000+ points, final round is triggered
  • All other players take one final turn
  • Highest score wins

📝 Notes

  • Hot Streak chaining creates high-risk, high-reward turns
  • Great variant for experienced Farkle players
  • Add a visual tracker or token to show combo level
  • Optional Rule: +500 bonus if you achieve a Hot Streak on 3 consecutive turns
